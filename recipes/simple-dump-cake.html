Simple Dump Cake

This is a very simple recipe with a minimum of 4 basic ingredients. You can add some more if you want, but it's a hit at potlucks even with just the 4.

Ingredients:

  • 1 Can of crushed pineapple, around 15-16~ oz.
  • 1 Can of cherry pie filling, around 15oz, same size ish of the pineapple
  • Salted Butter, I usually do one stick. I like Kerrygold but you can pick anything.
  • Yellow Cake, I typically get one box of Duncan Hines or Krustyaz, or you can do a gluten free box if needed. I believe pillsbury makes one.

Note:

  • You'll want a 9x13 pan. A 9x9 can be fine but it will be thicker of course. If you don't have one at home they are really cheap for disposable ones
  • If no one has a nut allergy, pecans are a popular 5th ingredient addition. I usually will top half the cake for people that do prefer

Steps:

  1. Pour the fruits in, the order doesn't really matter.
  2. Mix them up to where they are evenly distributed
  3. Spread out the yellow cake on top, try to make it as flat as possible
  4. Melt the butter and then pour it on top, try to spread it evenly.
  5. Bake for about 20 minutes at 425 degrees, keep an eye on it. Pull it out when it's golden.
  6. Take it out and keep it warm or put it in the fridge to cool overnight.
  7. Serve by itself or warm it up and top it with vanilla icecream for an extra treat!
